1.	Write 5 meta tags and their usages.
Ans:Name of 5 meta tags are given below: 
A.	Charset
B.	Description
C.	Keywords
D.	Author
E.	Viewport

Meta tags are used inside <head> tag. For example- 
<head>
  <meta charset="UTF-8">
  <meta name="description" content="Online Daily Shopping">
  <meta name="keywords" content="HTML, CSS, JavaScript">
  <meta name="author" content="Palash Halder">
  <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial- 
  scale=1.0">
</head>
